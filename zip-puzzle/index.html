2. Privacy Policy

2.1 General Information

This Privacy Policy explains how Bip Puzzle handles information when you use the app. Bip Puzzle is designed to collect as little personal data as reasonably possible while still providing core game features such as game progress, settings, leaderboards, Daily Challenge features, unlockable items, analytics, and optional rewarded advertising.

The app does not require you to create a personal account with your real name, email address, or password.

Bip Puzzle may display ads, including rewarded ads. Ads may be provided by Google AdMob. Depending on your region and your privacy choices, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

The current version does not offer real-money in-app purchases.

2.2 Data Processed by the App

Depending on how you use the app, the following data may be processed:

  • Anonymous user ID created by Firebase Authentication
  • Optional player name entered by the user
  • Leaderboard scores, completion times, resets, ranking data, and related game data
  • Daily Challenge winner messages entered by the user
  • Game progress, unlocked birds, unlocked themes, eggs balance, tutorial status, settings, and preferences
  • Analytics events, such as level starts, level completions, level exits, time spent on levels, hint usage, reset usage, Daily Challenge progress, and rewarded ad interactions
  • Technical app information, such as app version, device type, operating system, app instance identifiers, diagnostics, crash-related data, and performance information
  • Advertising-related information processed by advertising partners, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers where permitted, IP address, approximate location derived from IP address, app interaction data, ad interaction data, and fraud-prevention data

Bip Puzzle does not intentionally collect your real name, phone number, email address, contacts, precise GPS location, photos, microphone data, or address book data through normal app usage.

If you contact support by email, your email address and the content of your message will be processed to respond to your request.

2.3 Local Storage on Your Device

Some data is stored locally on your device. This may include game progress, unlocked birds or themes, eggs balance, settings, tutorial progress, privacy choices, and other preferences.

This data is used to make the app work correctly and to remember your choices.

Deleting the app from your device may remove local app data from that device. It may not automatically delete online leaderboard entries or other data that was already submitted online.

2.4 Firebase

Bip Puzzle uses Google Firebase services to provide online features and app functionality.

Firebase may be used for:

  • Anonymous authentication
  • Online leaderboards
  • Daily Challenge features
  • Storing player progress or app-related game data
  • Analytics and app usage measurement
  • Diagnostics, stability, and performance improvement

Firebase may process technical information such as device information, app instance identifiers, authentication identifiers, timestamps, diagnostic information, and event data.

Leaderboard data may include your anonymous user ID, optional player name, score, completion time, resets, ranking data, and winner message.

Firebase is provided by Google. More information about Google's privacy practices can be found here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.5 Analytics

Bip Puzzle may use Firebase Analytics or similar analytics services to understand how the app is used and to improve gameplay, difficulty, stability, and user experience.

Analytics events may include, for example:

  • App starts
  • Level starts
  • Level completions
  • Level exits
  • Time spent on levels
  • Hint usage
  • Reset usage
  • Daily Challenge starts and completions
  • Rewarded ad requests, starts, completions, and failures
  • Country or region information derived from technical data such as IP address

Analytics data is used in aggregated form to understand app usage. It is not intended to identify you by your real name.

Where required, analytics storage or related processing may depend on your consent or privacy settings.

2.6 Advertising and Rewarded Ads

Bip Puzzle may display ads, including rewarded ads, provided by Google AdMob or other advertising partners.

Rewarded ads are optional. If you choose to watch a rewarded ad, you may receive an in-app reward after successful completion of the ad. Rewards may not be granted if an ad fails, is interrupted, cannot be verified, or is unavailable.

Advertising partners may process information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers, where permitted
  • IP address
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • App interaction data
  • Ad interaction data
  • Diagnostic and fraud-prevention data
  • Consent and privacy choice information

This information may be used for purposes such as:

  • Showing ads
  • Providing rewarded ads
  • Limiting ad frequency
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Preventing fraud and abuse
  • Improving ad delivery
  • Showing personalized ads, where you have given consent and where permitted by law
  • Showing non-personalized or contextual ads where applicable

In some regions, including the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the app may ask for your consent before using certain advertising features, personalized ads, or related storage and identifiers.

You can manage advertising and privacy choices in the app's Privacy Settings where available.

More information about Google's privacy practices can be found here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.7 Apple App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, the app may ask for permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt if tracking permission is required for certain advertising or measurement purposes.

If you do not allow tracking, certain identifiers such as the Apple advertising identifier may not be available for tracking purposes. You may still see ads, but they may be less personalized.

Apple's own privacy settings on your device may also affect advertising identifiers and tracking permissions.

2.8 Leaderboards and Winner Messages

If you use online leaderboard features, your score, completion time, resets, anonymous user ID, optional player name, and ranking data may be stored online.

If you submit a Daily Challenge winner message, that message may be visible to other players. Winner messages are intended as short, public game messages. They are not a private chat feature.

Please do not enter personal information, contact details, offensive content, links, or sensitive information into your player name or winner message.

The app provider may remove, hide, or reset content that appears abusive, harmful, illegal, offensive, misleading, or inappropriate.
