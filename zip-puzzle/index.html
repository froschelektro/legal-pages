2. Privacy Policy

2.1 General information

This Privacy Policy explains how Zip Puzzle handles information when you use the app. Zip Puzzle is designed to collect as little personal data as reasonably possible while still providing core game features such as game progress, settings, leaderboards, Daily Challenge features, unlockable items, and optional rewarded ad features.

The current TestFlight version may use test ads only. Test ads are used for development and testing and do not generate real advertising revenue.

The current version does not offer real-money in-app purchases. If real ads or in-app purchases are enabled in a future public release, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly before those features are made available.

2.2 Data processed by the app

Depending on how you use the app, the following data may be processed:

  • Anonymous user ID created by Firebase Authentication
  • Optional player name entered by the user
  • Leaderboard scores, completion times, resets, and ranking data
  • Daily Challenge winner messages entered by the user
  • Game progress, unlocked birds, unlocked themes, eggs balance, settings, tutorial status, and preferences
  • Technical app information, such as app version, device information, diagnostics, and crash-related data

The app does not require you to create a personal account with your real name, email address, or password.

2.3 Local storage on your device

Some data is stored locally on your device. This may include game progress, unlocked birds or themes, eggs balance, settings, tutorial progress, and other preferences. This data is used to make the app work correctly and to remember your choices.

2.4 Firebase

Zip Puzzle uses Google Firebase services to provide online features such as anonymous authentication and leaderboards.

Firebase may process technical information such as device information, app instance identifiers, authentication identifiers, timestamps, and diagnostic information. Leaderboard data may include your anonymous user ID, optional player name, score, time, resets, ranking data, and winner message.

Firebase is provided by Google. More information about Google's privacy practices can be found here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.5 Leaderboards and winner messages

If you use online leaderboard features, your score, time, resets, anonymous user ID, optional player name, and related ranking data may be stored online.

If you submit a Daily Challenge winner message, that message may be visible to other players. Please do not enter personal information, contact details, offensive content, links, or sensitive information into your player name or winner message.

Winner messages are intended as short, public game messages. They are not a private chat feature.

2.6 Advertising

The current TestFlight version may use test ads only. Test ads are used for development and testing and do not generate real advertising revenue.

If real ads are enabled in a future public release, Zip Puzzle may display ads, including rewarded ads. Ads may be provided by Google AdMob. In that case, advertising partners may process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interaction data, and other technical information for purposes such as ad delivery, fraud prevention, measurement, and, where permitted, personalized advertising.

If required in your region, the app may ask for your consent before using certain advertising features. You may also be able to manage privacy choices in the app settings, if this feature is available.

More information about Google's privacy practices can be found here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.7 In-app purchases

The current version does not offer real-money in-app purchases.

If in-app purchases are added in a future release, Zip Puzzle may offer optional digital items such as eggs, birds, themes, hints, resets, or other in-game features. Payments would be processed by the app store platform, such as the Apple App Store or Google Play. The app provider would not receive full payment details such as credit card numbers.

This Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use will be updated before real-money in-app purchases are enabled.

2.8 Purposes of processing

Data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Providing and operating the app
  • Saving game progress and settings
  • Providing leaderboards and Daily Challenge features
  • Displaying winner messages
  • Providing unlockable items and in-game rewards
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, cheating, and technical misuse
  • Improving stability, performance, and user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations

2.9 Legal bases

Where the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, processing may be based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Your consent, where required
  • Performance of a contract, where processing is necessary to provide the app features you use
  • Legitimate interests, such as app security, fraud prevention, basic diagnostics, and app functionality
  • Legal obligations, where processing is required by law

2.10 Data sharing

Data may be shared with service providers that are necessary to operate the app, such as:

  • Google Firebase
  • Google AdMob, if real ads are enabled in a future release
  • Apple App Store or Google Play, if in-app purchases are enabled in a future release
  • Other technical service providers required to operate, distribute, or improve the app

The app provider does not sell your personal data.

2.11 International data transfers

Some service providers may process data in countries outside your country of residence. Where required, appropriate safeguards are used according to applicable data protection laws.

2.12 Data retention

Data is kept only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Local app data may remain on your device until you delete the app or reset the app data. Online leaderboard data may remain stored until it is deleted, overwritten, or no longer needed.

2.13 Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, withdraw consent, or request data portability.

To exercise your rights, please contact: kontakt@fabian-messer.de

You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection authority.

2.14 Children

Zip Puzzle is not specifically directed at children. The app does not knowingly request children to provide personal information such as real names, addresses, or email addresses.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that we can review and delete the information where appropriate.

2.15 Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

2.16 Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time, for example when app features, service providers, or legal requirements change. The latest version will be available on this page.

3. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of data associated with your anonymous leaderboard profile by contacting: kontakt@fabian-messer.de

Please include enough information to identify the data you want deleted. If the app provides a “Delete my leaderboard data” feature in the settings, you can also use that feature.

Deleting the app from your device may remove local app data from that device. It may not automatically delete online leaderboard entries or other data that was already submitted online.
