2. Privacy Policy

2.1 General Information

This Privacy Policy explains how Atomic Highway handles information when you use the app.

Atomic Highway is designed to collect as little personal data as reasonably possible. The app does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, photos, microphone data, or other directly identifying information.

The app may display optional rewarded advertisements. Ads may be provided by Google AdMob. Depending on your region and privacy choices, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

2.2 Data Processed by the App

Atomic Highway itself does not collect or store personal user profiles, leaderboard data, messages, or online game statistics.

The app may store information locally on your device, including:

  • Game progress
  • Unlocked content
  • Settings and preferences
  • Gameplay-related information required for the app to function

2.3 Advertising and Rewarded Ads

Atomic Highway may display ads, including rewarded ads, provided by Google AdMob or other advertising partners.

Advertising partners may process information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers, where permitted
  • IP address
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • App interaction data
  • Ad interaction data
  • Diagnostic and fraud-prevention information
  • Consent and privacy choice information

This information may be used for:

  • Providing advertisements
  • Showing rewarded ads
  • Measuring advertising performance
  • Preventing fraud and abuse
  • Improving advertising services
  • Showing personalized ads where consent has been provided

More information about Google's privacy practices:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.4 Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)

Atomic Highway may use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage consent choices regarding advertising.

2.5 Apple App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, Atomic Highway may request permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework if tracking permission is required for certain advertising purposes.

3. Data Deletion

Atomic Highway does not create online user accounts or store personal user profiles.

Locally stored game data can usually be deleted by removing the app from your device.

4. Children

Atomic Highway is not specifically directed at children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Sharing
